About Joshua

Joshua A. Clennon is a dedicated community leader, entrepreneur, and advocate for equitable change. With extensive experience in real estate, politics, and grassroots organizing, Joshua has built a reputation as a visionary leader committed to empowering underserved communities and driving impactful initiatives.

As the founder of UMDI, a dynamic real estate firm, and New Voices Now, a nonprofit organization focused on increasing civic engagement and building grassroots political power, Joshua is at the forefront of addressing systemic disparities in housing, education, and public safety. Through these ventures, he works tirelessly to advance opportunities for historically marginalized communities, emphasizing sustainability and equity.

In the political sphere, Joshua's leadership extends to his work with Harlem Democrats for Change and other civic initiatives aimed at fostering accountability and transparency in government. As a former candidate for public office, he understands the challenges communities face and is committed to addressing disparities in public policy and representation.

A proud father and husband, Joshua is passionate about creating a legacy of opportunity and justice. His work is driven by the belief that true progress comes from engaging and uplifting people at the grassroots level while holding systems of power accountable.

Whether in the boardroom or on the streets of Harlem, Joshua A. Clennon is a bold advocate for sustainable, community-focused solutions.
